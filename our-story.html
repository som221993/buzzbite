Our Story

From late-night hustle to game-changing chew.

I still remember the moment Buzz Bite was born. It was 3:17 AM. I was wired, exhausted, and hunched over my laptop trying to finish a design project that was due at sunrise. Three cups of coffee deep and already feeling the jittery crash — I realized something had to change.

My roommate Tanisha walked in half-asleep for her 5 AM lab prep. She grabbed a stick of gum, looked at my coffee cup graveyard, and casually muttered:

“You know… someone should just put caffeine in gum.”

That offhand comment lit a spark. What if energy didn’t have to come with the crash, the mess, or the bitter aftertaste?

From that night on, we became obsessed. We started researching how caffeine works, how fast it absorbs through the gums, what flavors hit right, and how we could make something that actually tastes good.

We weren’t building a product — we were solving a problem we lived every single day:

How do we stay sharp, focused, and in control… without sacrificing health or convenience?

And that’s how Buzz Bite was born.

Today, Buzz Bite isn’t just gum. It’s a no-BS energy source for people like us — who are always on the move, building, grinding, dreaming.

We made Buzz Bite because we needed it. And now, it’s yours.

— The Buzz Bite Crew ⚡
